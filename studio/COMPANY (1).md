# About the Company

---

## Vision

We are building a company designed to build companies.

The problem we've set out to solve is building and validating products at speed with minimal resourcing. Our foundation is a lean, AI-native internal operating system. From day one, we prioritize automation, structured experimentation, and intelligent tooling.

Human effort is reserved for judgment, creativity, and high-leverage decisions. Everything that can be systemized, will be.

---

## What We Are

A venture studio. We don't build one product — we build a machine for repeatable product creation.

Our internal backbone (Warp) — processes, data flows, AI orchestration, and execution frameworks — is the core asset. Every product we build must contribute to and benefit from it. Once proven, product velocity compounds.

We are R&D-first. We experiment aggressively. We empower members. We move fast.

---

## The Backbone — Warp

Warp is our internal operating system. It is not a product. It is the infrastructure, automation layer, AI orchestration, shared data, and playbook library that every product runs on. See `WARP.md` for full detail.

## Open Founding Positions

**Chief Product Officer & Co-Founder** (30+ hrs/week, 20% equity)
Owns the product roadmap across all product lines. Leads ideation, validation, and execution. Maintains sprint cadence and delivery discipline. Accountable for product-market fit progress.

**Product Co-Founder / Executive Co-Founder** (20 hrs/week, 10% equity)
Contributes to product ideation and feature design. Supports roadmap planning, user research, and validation. Promotion path to CPO available at 30+ hrs/week.

**Chief Technical Officer & Co-Founder** (30+ hrs/week, 20% equity)
Owns technical architecture and stack. Leads engineering execution and standards. Accountable for reliability, security, and scalability.

**Technical Co-Founder** (20 hrs/week, 10% equity)
Contributes to architecture and technical design. Supports infrastructure and security planning. Promotion path to CTO available.

**Strategic Technical Advisor** (5 hrs/week, low equity)
Provides high-level technical guidance. Reviews major architectural decisions on request.

## Operating Principles

- The backbone is the product. Everything else is output.
- Automate on second repetition.
- AI-native by default — Claude is the first tool considered, not the last.
- Human judgment for what matters. Systems for everything else.
- Every product feeds the data layer. No silos.
- Experiment fast, kill fast, compound learnings.
